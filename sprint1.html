To enhance the usability and effectiveness of the Stock market analysis tool for better investment decisions

Impact

Suboptimal investment decisions due to limited understanding of market dynamics High user dissatisfaction rate indicating a need for improvement in the user experience

Solution Overview

Project Aim: To improve the Stock market analysis tool and empower users to make informed investment decisions, leading to increased satisfaction and potential investment success. Our commitment to reliability extends to the data we use, sourced from trusted platforms like Yahoo Finance, ensuring users have access to accurate and up-to-date market information.
